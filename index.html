About Scholl

Welcome to Our School

ST. XAVIERS SCHOOL, KARUMUNDA was established in 1995 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in BALLIGUDA block of KANDHAMAL district of Orissa. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Odia is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road.
